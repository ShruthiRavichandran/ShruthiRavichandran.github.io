Welcome to Tunnel Racing!

You will be dropped into a random location in the tunnels and have a target building to reach.

Use your mouse to drag around the screen to see the full 360 view of your location and use the arrow keys to move forward, backward, left, and right.

Race to the target building as fast as possible!




This game was made with love for my Art and Design thesis project! I have never built a website or game before, so please excuse any bugs!

If you have any suggestions for how to improve or find any bugs, please submit them here and I will try my best to fix them!
